Smartphones have taken over most of our screen time. Even tablets are being designed to replace laptops. Even as the ubiquitous bricks and slabs are getting more and more powerful each week, there’s always a need for a real computer. For most people, this typically means a laptop.

Even the most digitally-forward folks will admit there’s no substitute for a keyboard and a big screen. Tasks like creating spreadsheets or presentations and editing photos or videos are far more easily accomplished on a laptop than on a tablet or mobile phone. But what laptop is right for you depends on a variety of factors: what exactly you plan on using it for, how often you intend to use it, and (most importantly) your budget.

Different types of laptop computers available in Croma

Whether you are a student or a professional, a laptop helps you easily do multiple tasks. You can get an AMD Radeon Graphics laptop for an ultimate gaming feel or good storage for storing large files. Stay in sync with the latest technology with a 2-in-1 laptop or experience a super smooth performance with a Windows laptop or a MacBook.

How to buy a laptop by utility?

Every person has their own ways and needs of using a laptop; hence, nowadays, laptops are designed that way. So, if gaming is your skill or you love playing games day and night, a powerful gaming laptops will help you keep up with the action. You can also find laptops for everyday use or laptops for entertainment for those who just need a laptop for basic requirements. If you are always on your toes with presentations and documents, then laptops for office use are for you.

How do you choose a laptop computer?

By their very nature, laptops are designed to be portable. A 15.6-inch screen size is standard in most laptops, but smaller screen sizes –12 to 14-inch laptop – make a laptop more portable. This is also the size that’s preferred by most: no matter whether you’re a student or a working professional. However, gamers and content creators seek higher specs and faster processors to keep up with their usage. 

The display also plays an important role when choosing a laptop. Several laptops offer a 4K resolution, with Full-HD and HD-Ready options. Selecting the right one is crucial since you’ll likely be staring at it for almost the entire day. Operating Systems or OS are also crucial since the user experience in the three major systems – Mac, Windows, and Chrome – vastly differs. While each of the three has its strengths, picking one often boils down to personal choice than which one is better. You can refer to our laptop buying guide to learn more about different factors when choosing a laptop.

What are some best Laptops for work and play?

Windows laptops are among the highest-selling machines across the world. Several manufacturers –HP and Dell to Lenovo and Asus – make Windows laptops. And while these are more common, Apple MacBooks are also getting popular for their sleek looks, shiny interface, and highly user-friendly behaviour. 2-in-1 Laptops are also getting more popular than ever as we find newer uses for our machines – to work and collaborate, binge-watch shows, and even read.

Similarly, as gaming is getting more mainstream, so are gaming laptops. Their high-powered processors and top-of-the-line display cards are ideal for live stream games and other uses such as editing 4K videos and high-resolution photos and even creating graphics.

What are the different laptop computers by price?

Laptop prices in India vary a lot and are segregated based on the features that every model offers. If you are low on budget, you can go for laptops below 30,000 that have good basic features to do the job. If your budget doesn’t hold you back, you can opt for laptops between 30,000 and 50,000 or 50,000 and 75,000. And lastly, if you need a laptop with high-end features and the latest advancements, you can go for laptops above 75,000, 1,00,000 or even above 2,00,000.

How to choose a laptop based on the processor?

Processors play a vital role in laptops for a smooth work process; hence, choosing the right one is essential. Get an i3 processor laptops or a Ryzen 3 or Ryzen 5 laptop for comfortable task handling to fulfil your basic computing needs. And if you’re looking for a laptop that does intensive tasks smoothly, then a Ryzen 9 or i9 processor laptops is just right for you.

How to choose a laptop based on RAM?

RAM comes into the picture when you need your laptop to work fast and perform best. Complete your everyday tasks quickly without lag with an 8GB RAM laptop which is good for gaming laptops and multitasking. If you are looking for an entry-level laptop for basic computing needs, a 4GB RAM laptop is for you. And a 32GB RAM laptops is for those who perform heavy-duty tasks and must switch between applications quickly without glitches.
