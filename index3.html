Factors to consider while buying a Mobile Phone

If you want to buy a smartphone, the first thing to decide is the operating system you may be comfortable
with -- Android or iOS. Designed only for iPhone, iOS is known for its speed and security. On the contrary,
Android mobiles are more common since they run on an open-source OS and are widely available. Several
brands such as Samsung, Oppo, and Xiaomi, among others, offer Android phones. Buy the latest Android
smartphone online at easy EMI

Some other important features to consider while choosing a mobile phone are its display size, RAM, storage
capacity, and battery life. A phone with a 5.5 – 6-inch HD or QHD display is usually an ideal option for most of
us. RAM is another crucial factor as it stores Operating System, apps, and data that are currently in use.
Where up to 4GB RAM phones are good for calling, basic web browsing, basic social media usage, 8GB
RAM mobile phones are ideal for heavy video watching, gaming, and e-reading. If you find yourself confused
about which Smartphone to choose, refer to our Mobile Buying Guide or #AskTheExperts at any Croma
store near you.

While Smartphones are the most popular phones today, some still like the old-school charm of a Feature phone
. With physical buttons and limited features, these mobile phones are the first choice of people who
want a no-fuss phone merely to stay in touch. The OG phone maker, Nokia continues to be a major player in
feature phones even today.
